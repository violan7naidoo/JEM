Meet The Team

Life is hectic right! We often fail to feed our bodies the nutrients we need and grab the easiest method...junk food! I did it myself and went on roller coaster diets! But then decided to live a healthier life and started cooking balanced meals for myself and my family. Someone asked me how do you maintain such a healthy lifestyle and that's where 'Yum-Foods' started preparing meals for fellow enthusiasts! Yumfoodsqatar focus on a well balanced, consistent and healthy lifestyle. After identifying the need in the community, Yumfoodsqatar prepare meals according to your specific goal, whether it is to lose weight, build muscle or simply a healthy happy lifestyle. *We don't do diet, we do nutrition* fueling your body with *well balanced nutritional food*. Mindful eating is key, therefor we pride in what we do, offering you fresh prepared meals delivered to your doorstep. *Become part of the Yum-Food family and make your health an investment!*
