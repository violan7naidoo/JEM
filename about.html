The Restaurant

Where we do Portuguese seafood and chicken to specific client requirements or we have our own range of set menu options. There are many delicious options on the menu at JEM, including a wide range of salads, starters, pizzas, meat and seafood dishes, plus pastas and desserts. We are strictly take away & delivery due to the pandemic. Begin your meal with an order of the mussels in garlic and cream sauce or the chicken liver peri-peri, then make the tough decision between one of Julio's amazing main courses. We recommend the full chicken and chips with 4 portuguese rolls & coke , the seafood platters, or the king size prawns. Julio's is known to get busy, so please book ahead to get your meal on time!

You can also place your order with Mr D and get your meal delivered to your doorstep.
